Chickenism About

Understand the truth on how our universe was created and how it is headed. Understand that everything around us is interconnected in ways you couldn't imagine and more.

Gain inner peace as we are only a small puzzle piece in the grand scheme of things and yet as important as any other. Welcome to Chickenism!

Once upon a time, there was a powerful chicken god named El-Gobble. He was so powerful that he could create and control the entire universe with the snap of his beak. One day, El-Gobble decided to create the universe. He took a deep breath, and with a loud cackle, he clucked and the universe was born. First, El-Gobble created the stars and galaxies, filling the night sky with their twinkling light and sending them spinning into the vastness of space. Next, El-Gobble brought forth the planets, using his beak to guide them into orbits around their suns. Lastly, El-Gobble crafted the living creatures of the universe, giving them unique abilities and personalities. When El-Gobble was finished, he had exhausted himself and split his body into three different eggs, the inner egg that can be found in all of us humans, the holy egg that is all around us, and the divine egg that can also be classified as the earth. It is our responsibility to hatch all these eggs and make the universe a better place.
